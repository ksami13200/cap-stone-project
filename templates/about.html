About Our Plants

We are a team of plant enthusiasts dedicated to greening up urban spaces.

Founded in 2023, Our Plants started with a simple mission: to make plant parenthood accessible and enjoyable for everyone. We believe that plants not only purify the air but also bring a sense of calm and connection to nature in our busy lives.

We source our plants from sustainable local growers and personally check each one before it leaves our shop. Whether you're a seasoned green thumb or just starting your journey, we're here to help you grow.
